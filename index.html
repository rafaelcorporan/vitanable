Frequently Asked Questions

How does a vitamins-based deodorant work?

Our formula works by neutralizing odor-causing bacteria while nourishing the skin with vitamins. Unlike conventional antiperspirants, we don't block sweat glands with aluminum compounds, allowing your body's natural detoxification process to continue while still providing effective odor protection.

Will I experience a "transition period" when switching?

Some people experience a brief adjustment period (2-3 weeks) when switching from conventional antiperspirants to natural deodorants. This is your body detoxifying and rebalancing after years of aluminum exposure. Our vitamin-enriched formula helps minimize this transition and supports skin health throughout the process.

How long does the product last?

With proper storage in a cool, dry place, our deodorant has a shelf life of 6-9 months, protected by the antioxidant properties of Vitamins C and E. Each stick typically lasts 2-3 months with regular daily use.

Is this product suitable for sensitive skin?

Yes! Our formula is specifically designed to be gentle on sensitive skin. We avoid common irritants like baking soda and use skin-soothing ingredients like shea butter and vitamin B3 that help strengthen the skin barrier and reduce irritation.

Are your products cruelty-free and vegan?

Our products are 100% cruelty-free. Our standard formula contains beeswax, but we offer a vegan option that uses candelilla wax instead. All other ingredients in both formulations are plant-based.
